About us

This is a work born from a research group of ISKO Italy on the mathematical structure of facet classifications. The link to the web resource is http://www.iskoi.org/penumbra/. It is a bibliography developed during the research space, starting from the book of Prolegomena S.R.Ranganathan and developing from works by Charles Sanders Peirce, to first order logic and propositional calculus, up to boundary logic. It ends with Derik Austin's work on PRECIS and John Sowa's work on conceptual graphs. In the repository it is possible to find some source code in javascript on the material treated in the bibliography. From a BTree implementation, to a rule Engine, to an advanced conditional probability calculator. There are 5 tracks:

  • track 1: Prolegomena Ranganathan
  • track 2: Charles Sanders Peirce
  • track ontolog: Propositional Calcolus
  • track lawsOfForm: Syllogism and boundary logic
  • track conceptualGraph: Conceptual graph and Precis

An in-depth study of chapter Q, R and S of the prolegomena of S.R.Ranganathan in which he explains the APUPA arrangement. The work began from the writing of Miksa Francis. In addition to dealing with fillign curves, it also covers the axiomatic aspect of subject strings, a subject very dear to S.R.Ranganathan.
